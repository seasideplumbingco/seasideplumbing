Terms & Conditions

The estimate provided is based on visible conditions at the time of inspection and the scope of work described. Any additional work required due to unforeseen conditions may result in additional charges and will be discussed with the customer prior to proceeding.

The following items are not included in this estimate or invoice unless specifically stated:

1. Drywall, Flooring & Wall Repairs

This estimate does not include any finish work such as drywall removal or repair, painting or texture matching, flooring removal or repair, or the removal and reinstallation of cabinets, trim, or other finish materials.

2. Structural or Framing Work

Structural modifications are not included. This includes cutting, repairing, or reinforcing joists, beams, studs, or other structural components beyond what is normally required for pipe routing.

3. Mold, Rot or Existing Damage

This estimate does not include remediation or repair of any mold, wood rot, water damage, pest damage, or other existing structural issues that may be discovered during the course of work.

4. Water Heater Work

Water heater repair, replacement, relocation, or modifications to gas or electrical connections are not included unless specifically stated in the scope of work.

Water heater temperature will be set to 120°F for safety. Any adjustments above 120°F must be performed by the owner. Seaside Plumbing & Mechanical assumes no liability for temperatures set above 120°F.

5. Plumbing Fixtures

Plumbing fixtures such as toilets, faucets, shower valves, sinks, tubs, trim kits, or other fixture components are not included unless specifically listed. If fixture installation is included, the cost of the fixtures themselves may not be included unless otherwise stated.

6. Drain or Sewer Line Work

Replacement or repair of sewer lines, drain lines, or vent piping is not included unless specifically listed in the scope of work.

7. Permits and Inspections

Permit fees, inspection fees, engineering requirements, or other regulatory costs are not included unless otherwise stated.

8. Accessibility Issues

Additional charges may apply if limited access conditions are encountered, including tight crawlspaces, limited attic access, obstructions such as ductwork, electrical lines, insulation, or other construction elements that restrict access to plumbing systems.

9. Concrete, Excavation or Landscaping Work

Breaking concrete slabs, tunneling, excavation, restoration of landscaping, concrete replacement, asphalt repair, sod replacement, irrigation repair, or other site restoration work is not included unless specifically outlined in the estimate.
